FDA Regulatory Reality — Feb 2026

The FDA cracked down hard in 2025-2026. Know the landscape before you buy anything.

  • BPC-157, TB-500, CJC-1295, Ipamorelin, AOD-9604 — Category 2 (Safety Concerns). Compounding pharmacies cannot legally compound these for human use.
  • Semaglutide, Tirzepatide, Sermorelin, Tesamorelin — FDA-approved. Available via prescription.
  • PT-141 — FDA-approved as Vyleesi (women HSDD). Off-label for men via physician.
  • GHK-Cu — OTC in topical skincare. Injectable is gray market.
  • Thymosin Alpha-1 — Approved in 35+ countries. Category 1 in US (eligible for compounding).
  • DSIP — Not FDA-approved. Research peptide only.

Bottom line: The "research chemical" loophole is closing. FDA referring mass-marketing compounders to DOJ. For legal and clean — go through a licensed telehealth clinic or prescribing physician for the approved ones. Gray market is on you.

The Peptides

1. BPC-157 — Body Protection Compound

Gray Market

Derived from stomach acid protein. Promotes tissue healing, angiogenesis, fibroblast activity, collagen remodeling. Downregulates inflammatory cytokines. The "Wolverine peptide" when stacked with TB-500.

Gut healing, tendon/ligament repair, muscle recovery, joint pain, neuroprotection, reduces inflammation

Very well-tolerated. Mild: fatigue during adaptation, injection site irritation, transient nausea

250-500 mcg/day subQ. 4-8 weeks on, 2-4 weeks off. Inject near injury site or abdomen for systemic

$30-60 per 5mg vial (research). Clinic was $150-400/mo when available

2. TB-500 — Thymosin Beta-4 Fragment

Gray Market

Synthetic Thymosin Beta-4. Promotes actin production, cell migration, blood vessel formation. Stacks synergistically with BPC-157 ("Wolverine Stack").

Muscle/tendon/ligament healing, cardiac tissue repair, hair regrowth, reduced scarring, flexibility

Mild: headache, nausea, injection site reactions, temporary lethargy. Generally well-tolerated

Loading: 750 mcg 2x/week for 4-6 weeks. Maintenance: 750 mcg 1x/week

$40-70 per 5mg vial (research grade)

3. CJC-1295 — Growth Hormone Releaser

Gray Market

Synthetic GHRH analog. Increases natural GH and IGF-1 secretion. "With DAC" version has longer half-life (days vs hours). Best paired with Ipamorelin.

Lean muscle, fat loss, improved sleep, faster recovery, skin quality, bone density

Water retention, numbness/tingling, joint pain, headache, flushing. Theoretical cancer concern (broad cell growth)

No DAC: 100-300 mcg before bed, 5 on / 2 off. With DAC: 2mg 1x/week. Combine with Ipamorelin

$35-60 per 2mg vial. Clinic protocols were $200-500/mo

4. Ipamorelin — Cleanest GH Secretagogue

Gray Market

Selective growth hormone secretagogue. Stimulates pituitary GH release without spiking cortisol or prolactin. Cleanest option available.

Lean muscle, fat loss, improved sleep, anti-aging, recovery. Fewer sides than other GH peptides

Mild: headache, lightheadedness, water retention. Less hunger than GHRP-6. Very clean

100-300 mcg subQ, 1-3x daily (often before bed). 8-12 week cycles. Best with CJC-1295

$30-50 per 5mg vial (research grade)

5. GHK-Cu — Copper Peptide

OTC (Topical)

Naturally occurring tripeptide bound to copper. Your body makes it — 200 ng/mL at age 20, drops to 80 ng/mL by 60. Stimulates collagen, antioxidant activity, wound healing. Outperformed vitamin C cream AND retinoic acid for collagen in a 12-week trial.

Skin tightening, wound healing, hair growth, reduced wrinkles, improved skin density/thickness

Very safe. No significant issues in topical or wound studies. Injectable: mild site reactions possible

Topical: 2-10% concentration (4% daily). Injectable: 1-2 mg/day subQ. Oral: 120 mcg 1-2x daily

Topical: $20-80. Injectable: $30-50 per 50mg vial. Buy topical from The Ordinary, NIOD, Amazon

6. Thymosin Alpha-1 — Immune Modulator

Compoundable (Cat 1)

Naturally produced in thymus gland. Regulates immune function. Enhances T-cells, dendritic cells, immune surveillance. Approved in 35+ countries as Zadaxin. One of the few wellness peptides still legally compoundable in the US.

Immune modulation, chronic infection support (Hep B/C, Lyme), cancer adjunct, reduced inflammation

Mild injection site reactions, rare rash. One of the safest peptides

1.6 mg subQ, 2x/week. Daily during acute illness. 4-12 week cycles

$150-400/mo compounding pharmacies. Research: $40-70 per 5mg vial

7. Semaglutide & Tirzepatide — GLP-1 Agonists

FDA Approved

Semaglutide (Ozempic/Wegovy): GLP-1 receptor agonist. Regulates blood sugar, slows gastric emptying, kills appetite.
Tirzepatide (Mounjaro/Zepbound): Dual GIP + GLP-1. Hits two receptors. Slightly better weight loss in head-to-head trials.

15-25%+ body weight loss, improved insulin sensitivity, cardiovascular benefits, appetite suppression

Nausea (very common early), vomiting, diarrhea, constipation. Serious: pancreatitis, gallbladder, thyroid warning. Muscle loss without resistance training

Sema: start 0.25mg/wk → up to 2.4mg. Tirz: start 2.5mg/wk → up to 15mg. Slow titration 4-8 weeks

Brand: $800-1,300/mo (insurance may cover). Compounded: $200-500/mo. Telehealth: $300-600/mo

8. PT-141 — Bremelanotide (Vyleesi)

FDA Approved

Melanocortin receptor agonist. Works in the brain on desire itself — not blood flow like Viagra/Cialis. Effective for men who don't respond to PDE-5 inhibitors.

Increased sexual desire/arousal (men and women). Works on desire pathways, not just blood flow

Nausea (40%), flushing (20%), injection site (13%), headache (11%). Skin darkening with repeated use. Pre-treat with Zofran

1.75 mg subQ, 45+ min before activity. Max 1x/24hr. Max 8 injections/month

Vyleesi brand: ~$900/mo. Compounded: $100-250/mo. Research: $25-40 per 10mg vial

9. Sermorelin — The Legal GH Option

Compoundable (Rx)

Synthetic GHRH (first 29 amino acids). Oldest and most studied GH peptide. Works with your body's natural feedback — won't suppress natural GH like exogenous HGH. More legally accessible than CJC/Ipamorelin.

Increased GH, improved sleep, fat loss, lean muscle, recovery, skin quality, energy

Mild: flushing, headache, dizziness, injection site reactions. Less water retention than direct GH

200-300 mcg subQ before bed. 3-6 month cycles. Some do 5 on / 2 off

$150-400/mo clinics/compounding. Research: $25-45 per 2mg vial

10. DSIP — Delta Sleep-Inducing Peptide

Research Only

Naturally occurring neuropeptide. First isolated 1977 from rabbit brains during slow-wave sleep. Enhances delta (deep) sleep in the hypothalamus.

Improved deep sleep, faster sleep onset, antioxidant, cortisol reduction, pain reduction

Fatigue/grogginess if dose too high, headache, dizziness. No significant adverse effects in studies

100-300 mcg subQ, 30-60 min before bed. Start 100 mcg, titrate up. 2-4 weeks on, 2 off

$25-50 per 5mg vial (research grade only)
